INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON RECENT TRENDS IN
DATA SCIENCE AND IT'S APPLICATIONS
MARCH 30-31, 2023
Organised by
DEPARTMENT OF DATA SCIENCE AND BUSINESS SYSTEMS
SRMIST, KATTANKULATHUR, TAMIL NADU, INDIA

ICRTDA 2023 is being organized as an in-person participation event to be held at SRM Institute of Science
and Technology, Kattankulathur on 30th and 31st, March 2023. The conference will focus on the Trends and
Recent Advances in Data Science and its Applications. Papers accepted for presentation will be published in
the Scopus Indexed Conference proceedings.
